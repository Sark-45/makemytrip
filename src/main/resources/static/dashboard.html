Pricing Rules & Transparency

Holiday Surge

During public holidays and festival seasons, prices increase by up to 20% automatically. Currently active: Diwali, Christmas, New Year window.

Demand-Based Pricing

When seat availability drops below 30%, prices rise dynamically. Below 10% availability triggers maximum pricing tier.

Off-Peak Discounts

Flights departing between 02:00–06:00 or on weekday middays receive automatic discounts of 10–15%.

Price Guarantee

Once you book, your price is locked. Use Price Freeze to hold a fare for up to 24 hours before committing to purchase.
